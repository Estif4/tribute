Nelson Mandela in 2013

In 2013, Nelson Mandela passed away at the age of 95. Mandela had been in poor health for several years and had been hospitalized numerous times, but his death was still a shock to many people around the world. Mandela's death was met with an outpouring of grief and tributes from people around the world, who recognized him as a global icon of peace and reconciliation. Mandela's legacy continues to inspire people around the world, and his life and work remain a shining example of what can be achieved through courage, commitment, and perseverance.
